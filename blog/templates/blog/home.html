What is IrinQA?

IrinQA's long-term vision is to be a safe haven for software testers and QA engineers to discuss all things tech.

For the short-term, it is a thought dump, a journal of sorts; the brainchild of a certain felis catus who operates as a QA Engineer by day, and reverts to true feline form at night.

Why the name "IrinQA"?

Because cats and bugs go well together.
